I am me

Important Information

On the vibrant 19th of May 2005,I,Kristine Joy Balazuela Igang, came into this world in the lively Manga District of Tagbilaran City. My family, a source of immense love and support, hails from the picturesque town of San Vicente in Maribojoc, Bohol. Early on, my journey into education began at Lincod Elementary School, where my fascination with numbers and the intricate world of mathematics took root. Moving forward, my time at Saint Vincent Institute allowed me to delve into my interests in accountancy and business management during my junior and senior high school years. Currently, I am pursuing my dreams of becoming a Computer Engineer at the esteemed university of Bohol, immersing myself in the exciting realm of technology.

However, my story extends beyond academic pursuits. I find immense joy in the realms of creativity. Whether immersed in literature as both a reader and writer, experimenting with flavors and creating culinary delights, or expressing myself through music, my creative outlets are diverse. Through it all, my family remains my anchor, providing unwavering support and inspiration. Together with my younger sister, Krissel Balazuela Igang, we navigate life journey, building a bond that weaves through time.

As my narrative unfolds, I am driven by passion and the love of my family. Ready to make a difference step by step, my aspirations revolve around leading a life of purpose and kindness. With technology as my ally, I aim to contribute to creating a brighter future. Filled with gratitude and a burning desire to leave a positive mark, I eagerly anticipate the adventures that await me on this extraordinary journey called life.
